Before you try the app

Tutorials

The first screen you see is the main screen,here you can choose to:

  1. Search the weather information of any city
  2. Take a look of the About page (the acclamation mark at top-right corner)
  3. Or try to ask the chatBot some questions!

After pressing the search button,you can see the information screen,you can:

  1. Check the weather statistics
  2. If there is something you don't understand,just tap on it,you can see extra information
  3. Or tap on the AR button at the bottom to see 3D models

After pressing the AR button,your camera will be turned on,in this screen:

  1. First,please do this in a area that doesn't have much things in it like a empty floor or clean table
  2. Move your phone as indicated in the animation shows to detect plane area
  3. If a plane area is detected,a group of white dots will appear,tap on those white dots once!
  4. after tapping,it might take a few seconds to render the 3D model,after it shows,you can move the model with one finger or rotate it with 2 fingers, try to find the answer of quiz in the model!
